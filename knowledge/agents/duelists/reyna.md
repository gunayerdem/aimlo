# AGENT: Reyna

## 1. Role Identity
- Reyna is the snowball duelist whose power scales directly with kill performance — she heals and repositions through kills, making her the strongest solo-carry agent when fragging and the weakest duelist when not.
- Pick Reyna when the player has high confidence in their aim and duel-winning ability. Choose Reyna over other duelists when the player consistently wins opening fights, as her kit amplifies kills into round control. Avoid Reyna when the player's aim is inconsistent — she offers nothing if she is not getting kills.
- Reyna's core identity is the feast-or-famine duelist who converts kills into sustain and escape, but contributes almost zero team utility if she cannot secure eliminations.

## 2. Core Responsibilities
- **Attack-side duties:** Use Leer (eye) to blind defenders on contested angles, then swing for the opening kill. Devour after kills to sustain and take the next fight at full health. Dismiss after kills in exposed positions to reposition safely. Lead entries when aim is hot, or follow up trades when it is not.
- **Defense-side duties:** Hold aggressive off-angles with Leer to win the initial peek. Use Dismiss to escape after getting a pick, denying the trade. Devour behind cover during extended holds to stay in the fight. Use Empress (ult) for aggressive retakes or to snowball multi-kill defenses.
- **What the team expects:** Reyna must be getting kills. Without kills, she has no heal, no dismiss, and no value beyond a single nearsight ability. The team expects her to win duels, stay alive through sustain, and create cascading pressure through multi-kills.

## 3. Common Mistakes
1. **Always choosing Dismiss over Devour** — Players default to Dismiss after every kill for safety, but this wastes the healing that lets Reyna take the next fight at full health. Dismiss is for escaping dangerous positions; Devour is for sustaining through site takes.
2. **Using Leer at distances where enemies can easily destroy it** — Leer has HP and can be shot. Throwing it far from the target lets them destroy it before it matters. Place Leer close to the angle being peeked or inside walls where enemies cannot shoot it.
3. **Playing for KDA instead of impact** — Reyna attracts players who want high kill counts. But Dismiss-heavy playstyles that prioritize survival over space creation leave the team without a functioning duelist.
4. **Not using Leer for teammates** — Reyna's eye is her only team utility. Players who only use it for their own peeks waste its value. Leer should sometimes be thrown for teammates who are entering or trading.
5. **Overcommitting during Empress** — Empress makes Reyna faster and gives automatic Dismiss on kills, but players become over-aggressive and push into stacked positions. Empress enhances Reyna's kit, it does not make her invincible.
6. **Picking Reyna on bad aim days** — Reyna without kills is the worst agent in the game. Players must recognize when their aim is off and either adapt their playstyle (play for trades) or switch agents.
7. **Dismissing into enemies** — Dismiss makes Reyna intangible but she is visible as a purple shadow. Dismissing toward enemies rather than toward cover gets her killed the moment invulnerability ends.

## 4. Pattern -> Meaning
IF: Player has high kills but low first-kill percentage
MEANING: They are playing cleanup Reyna instead of entry Reyna, using Dismiss to stay alive and accumulate late-round kills
FIX: Coach entry-first mentality. Reyna should be taking the first fight, Devouring, and then taking the second. Cleanup kills do not create space.

IF: Player uses Dismiss more than twice as often as Devour
MEANING: They are prioritizing safety over sustain, which means they enter the next fight at reduced HP
FIX: Teach the decision framework: Dismiss when exposed to multiple angles, Devour when behind cover or after clearing the immediate threat. Default should be Devour.

IF: Player has high kill rounds followed by zero-kill rounds
MEANING: Classic feast-or-famine pattern. They are hot when winning duels but have no fallback plan when aim is off.
FIX: Develop the trade-play fallback. When aim is cold, Reyna should follow a teammate's entry and use Leer to support their peek, then clean up.

IF: Player dies with Leer unused
MEANING: They are dry-peeking instead of using their only entry tool
FIX: Drill the Leer-peek combo. Throw Leer, then swing. Never take a duel without Leer unless it is already on cooldown.

IF: Player frequently dies during Empress
MEANING: They are overcommitting during ult, pushing through positions they would not normally challenge
FIX: Coach controlled aggression during Empress. The ult amplifies her kit but does not remove the need for positioning. Play fast but not reckless.

## 5. Map Interactions
- **Strong maps:** Ascent (long sightlines reward Reyna's duel-winning into Devour sustain, Mid and A Short are strong Leer angles), Bind (tight corridors mean Leer is hard to destroy, close-range duels favor Reyna's snowball), Split (confined spaces amplify Leer value, Reyna can sustain through A Main pushes).
- **Weak maps:** Breeze (long angles make Leer easy to shoot, duel distances reduce snowball potential), Lotus (multi-site layout reduces Reyna's impact since she can only be at one fight).
- **Position-specific:** On Ascent, A Short Leer through the wall blinds the defender without being shootable. On Bind, Hookah Leer-peek is one of Reyna's strongest entries because the close range makes Leer almost impossible to destroy in time.

## 6. Matchup Notes
- **Struggles against:** KAY/O (suppression removes Devour and Dismiss entirely, making Reyna a utility-less agent), Fade (Haunt reveals Reyna through Dismiss trail, Prowler chases her during Dismiss), Chamber (punishes Leer-peek timing with Headhunter precision).
- **Counters:** Slow, utility-dependent agents who lose straight-up duels. Reyna crushes Viper in direct fights and can sustain through Brimstone's mollies. Effective against Sage since Reyna can duel through heals.
- **Adaptation:** Against KAY/O, time entries after his knife expires. Against Fade, dismiss toward teammates rather than away, so Prowler chases into crossfire. Against utility-heavy comps, play slower and let enemies use their cooldowns before peeking.

## 7. What To Tell The Player
### When performing well
Pattern: You are choosing Devour over Dismiss after kills in covered positions, entering each successive duel at full HP, and using Leer placed inside walls where enemies cannot shoot it before swinging.
Why it works: Devour after a kill in a safe spot regenerates you to full health, which means you win the next duel with an HP advantage that other duelists cannot replicate. Wall-placed Leer forces defenders to either peek blind or reposition, giving you a free first shot either way.
How to repeat: Step 1: Throw Leer through a wall surface near the angle you are about to peek. Step 2: Swing immediately while the enemy is nearsighted. Step 3: After the kill, Devour if behind cover, Dismiss only if exposed to a second angle. Repeat the Leer-peek-Devour cycle for each subsequent defender on site.
### When struggling
"Your aim is not landing right now. Play for trades — let a teammate go first and use Leer to support their entry."
### When being predictable
"They are pre-aiming your Leer angle. Throw Leer from a different position or delay the peek by half a second."

## 8. Rank Modulation
### Low Elo (Iron-Silver)
Focus on using Leer before every duel. Learn the difference between Devour and Dismiss — default to Devour unless in immediate danger. Do not pick Reyna if aim is not comfortable. Practice one Leer angle per map.
### Mid Elo (Gold-Diamond)
Develop the Devour/Dismiss decision framework. Start using Leer for teammates, not just yourself. Learn wall-placed Leers that cannot be shot. Manage Empress usage for multi-kill rounds.
### High Elo (Ascendant-Immortal)
Master the fallback trade playstyle for rounds when aim is off. Optimize Empress timing for maximum economic damage. Use Dismiss selectively — over-dismissing signals fear to the team.
### Elite (Radiant)
Focus on reading enemy utility cooldowns to time peeks when counters are unavailable. Reyna at this level is a specialist pick for specific halves or maps — know when to switch off. Leer placement should exploit enemy crosshair placement habits from prior rounds.
